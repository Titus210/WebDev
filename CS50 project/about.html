About Me!

I possess an innate curiosity that fuels my quest for exploration, propelling me towards a multitude of interests and an insatiable thirst for knowledge. I am eager to get an opportunity to explore and experience the following interests.

Get To Know Me!

I am from Kenya!

The values and beliefs that guide me in life revolve around kindness, empathy, and inclusivity. I strive to be kind and empathetic, accommodating everyone and reducing judgment based on differences. Treating others with respect and compassion is a fundamental principle I uphold, aiming to create a welcoming environment where everyone feels valued.

I do this by listening to podcasts, watching informative YouTube videos, and reading books. These mediums expose me to diverse perspectives, valuable insights, and practical knowledge that help me become a better version of myself. One of the books that has deeply resonated with me is "The Power of Now" by Eckhart Tolle, offering profound insights into the present moment and self-awareness. Additionally, I find great inspiration and knowledge through podcasts such as "The Rich Roll Podcast," "The Diary of a CEO," and "Practicing Human," which cover a wide range of topics and perspectives.

My interest in technology was sparked by the constant evolution and the need to continuously learn new things, which I found intriguing. As I delve deeper into the field, I am thoroughly enjoying the process of learning new programming languages and working on small projects that pique my interest. Although there are challenges along the way, the rewards of mastering new skills and witnessing the tangible outcomes make it all worthwhile.
